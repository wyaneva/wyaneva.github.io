RESEARCH INTERESTS

My research interests are in the areas of software engineering, software testing, parallel architectures, programming models and compilers.

My PhD work focuses on developing novel methods to automate and accelerate the execution of large test suites by running tests in parallel on GPU architectures.

My supervisors are Dr Ajitha Rajan and Dr Christophe Dubach.
