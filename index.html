About Me

I am a PhD student at the CDT in Pervasive Parallelism at the University of Edinburgh. My background is in High Performance Computing and I hold an MSc from the Edinburh Parallel Computing Centre.

I also have three years of experience as a software engineer in industry.

Research Interests

My research interests are in the areas of parallel architectures and parallel programming models and their potential application to software testing. In particular, I am looking into ways to accelerate the execution of large application test suites by running test cases in parallel on heterogeneous architectures.

My supervisors are Dr Ajitha Rajan and Dr Christophe Dubach.
