VANYA YANEVA

I am a PhD candidate in Software Engineering at the University of Edinburgh. My background is in High Performance Computing and I hold an MSc from the Edinburgh Parallel Computing Centre.

I also have three years of experience as a software engineer in industry.

RESEARCH INTERESTS

My research interests are in the areas of software testing, software engineering, parallel architectures, programming models and compilers.

My PhD thesis is on novel methods for acceleration of large test suites by running tests in parallel on GPU threads.

My supervisors are Dr Ajitha Rajan and Dr Christophe Dubach.
